About Me

I majored in Mathematics and Economics at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, but I have had an interest in programming ever since the Lego Mindstorms robotics set I had as a child. I began to realize in college that I really enjoyed the thrill of completing a working program, and my first significant job out of college involved using SQL to write reports for a company then known as FDH Engineering. My talents rapidly developed into data warehouseing and SSAS cube development, and I got my first taste of javascript building a single page application for company budgeting.

After two years, it was time to move on, and I have been working in a team of analysts at Cisco with a focus on worldwide sales enablement, where I serve as the SSAS cube and MDX expert.

This website is my first public work, and you can check out more details about its creation on the about this website page. In my free time I have been dabbling in meanjs, the project used to architect this website, linux servers, and android development. I like to expand my knowledge and I love learning new things. Most important, however, is spending time with my wonderful fiance, Ms. Ashley Mayle: riding bikes, grilling out on the back porch, and playing with our friendly, toilet-trained cat, Mufasa.
